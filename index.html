Mumps cases in central Ohio

At Ohio State University, the virus has affected 108 students, 15 staff members, two family members, and 24 with strong ties to the university community, with most affected OSU-affiliated persons living in Columbus. 91 females and 58 males are affected at OSU, from three to 58 years of age. The university-linked cases include residents in Columbus, Athens, Belmont, Delaware, Fairfield, Franklin, Hamilton, Licking, and Pickaway counties.1
